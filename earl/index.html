Takeaways

#1: Go beyond word clouds to interpret and summarize feedback.

#2: Vectorize your unstructured data through tokenization and a document term matrix.

#3: Incorporate structured data to analyze unstructured data.

#4: Identify parts of speech and tf-idf to focus on the most intersting text.

#5: Utilize sentiment defined locally and globally to signal objective feedback.

#6: Make use of the available R packages.

#7: Don't be afraid to try your own approach.

#8: Have fun.
